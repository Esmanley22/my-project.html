Why I Chose Coding

The Big Why

To be quite honest I jump into a lot of different things because I love to learn new topics and ways to make money, and it's always really exciting when I can implement them. Before I moved and bought my ticket into this course I did stocks and crypto which I was decent at, however the learning curve costs a lot of pain initially. My old coworker was telling me about this course and I realized that I could constantly keep learning but never know everything. I like the challenge that presents. Also, this course and the knowledge that I can absorb here is a huge step out of the industry I currently work in, which excites me.

Opportunity

Challenges and Chances

I mentioned before that I like challenges. I feel the need to elaborate on that a bit more. The reason why I like the challenges this industry presents is more directed towards the lack of knowledge than the knowledge learned itself. I currently work in inventory as a lead/trainer, and have corrected multiple warehouses' inventory. The situation that I seem to come across the most, is that there is a lack of progression within companies once you learn enough to succeed and do well within your department. Reason being is that they want to keep you where it benefits them. I mentioned doing this course to one of the corporate people at my work and they already started to talk about getting me in their department. This course has already started opening doors for me within my career that weren't open before. The fact that I won't ever learn everything there is to coding is exciting too. I'm excited for the opportunities to come from learning here.
